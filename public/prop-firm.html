Automated A-Book Dealing System

Real funding. Automated A-book execution. Smart contract payouts.

Learn More on FundedHive

FundedHive's unique market execution system routes funded account trades to the real live market when the account is in profit. If the account drops more than -1% below the initial balance, the system automatically switches to B-book until you return above the starting level. To avoid unnecessary switching, keep a small profit buffer at all times.

START Live Capital

REACTIVATION Activate A-Book after reaching initial balance
06
RESULT Switch to A-Book — real profits can now be earned

$5,000 Account — AADS in Action

You start a funded account with $5,000 on A-Book (AADS). Here's how the system responds to different scenarios:

✅ Profitable +$250

You grow the account to $5,250. The system sees you're above the initial balance and keeps you on A-Book. You can withdraw profits instantly via the smart contract.

⚠️ -1% Trigger −$50

Account drops to $4,949 (-1.02%). Positions are closed. System switches to B-book. Any further B-book profits cannot be withdrawn — they convert to Hive Coins.

↩️ Recovery Back to $5,000+

You trade the account back above $5,000 in B-book. Once the balance reaches the initial level, A-Book reactivates and real withdrawable profits resume.
